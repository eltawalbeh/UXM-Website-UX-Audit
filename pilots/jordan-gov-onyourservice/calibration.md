# UXM Library Calibration — Pilot 002

**Pilot:** Jordan.gov / بخدمتكم public service-submission form  
**Date:** 14 July 2026

## Adopted calibration

Added `public_service_submission` to `data/uxm-checkpoints.v1.json`.

The bundle is suggested when a public service accepts a complaint, suggestion, report, or request containing personal details and/or attachments. It combines form clarity, dependency guidance, validation, privacy-at-point-of-collection, Arabic/English widget parity, accessibility, mobile, and perceived performance checks.

## Cross-pilot confirmation

The Tawasal and Jordan.gov pilots independently exposed the same three high-value audit themes:

1. Privacy context belongs at the point of personal-data collection.
2. RTL/bilingual QA must include third-party controls and upload widgets.
3. Transactional public-service flows require task focus over decorative hero/support content.
